A Brief Letter

Hello and welcome to my personal website.

I am a university language instructor, faculty trainer, and life-long learner. My career is one of my passions and I constantly strive to provide the highest quality service to all whom I work with.

In January 2017, I became the Instructional Technology Specialist in the Intensive English Language Program at Temple University. In this position, I work with learning software on a daily basis and seek out new opportunities and resources within the rapidly growing educational technology industry.

I invite you to learn more about my background and current work by visiting the various pages on this site. You may also find the Resources section useful to your own teaching and learning.

Thank you for taking the time to visit.

Kind Regards,
Sam
